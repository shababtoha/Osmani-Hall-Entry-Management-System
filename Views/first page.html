Entry Register of Osmani Hall

The website is about the digitalization of entry exit system of Osmany Hall. The exact time of arrival and departure of students will be automatically registered and if any occurrences appear, higher authority will be readily informed.

about

MIST is a residential institute. Right now MIST has one main Student Hall named “Osmany Hall” and 6 extension halls. The main hall was established in 2006 and the extension were in 2015. Osmany Hall is an 8 storied building with female and male wings, each wing having a capacity of 260 students. There are 20 staffs for each wing. The hall building accommodates a number of offices to facilitate the administrative works. Both Male and Female Wing has two seven storied extension building each accommodating additional 500 students. Therefore, the total seat capacity of the Hall is 1220 students (male and female). Osmany Hall is well-known for its structural beauty, residential facilities and the security of the students compared to the other hall in Bangladesh.A digitization of the current manual entry register is being developed that will keep track of the records digitally which will ensure the safety and security of the resident students of Osmani hall.
